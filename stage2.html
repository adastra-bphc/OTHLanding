Stage 2

In your determined pursuit of the mission entrusted by the Jedi Master, you stand before the iconic R2-D2 inside the enemy base. Its droid body is a canvas of tales, adorned with various etchings, a big 64 spray painted on his face, bearing witness to countless galactic adventures.
R2-D2's dome-shaped head, scarred by countless battles, now projects two paragraphs. The first paragraph contains an enigmatic collection of characters, intriguingly resembling a code,
while the second is a sprawling passage marked by a single, imposing question mark,
"Do you know where you're going, who you are and what lies beyond your short sighted purposes young jedi ?"
enticing you to delve deeper into its depths.
Despite the unusual nature of this task, R2-D2's beeps and chirps persist, a continuous repetition of "L V W VI" that offers no immediate clarity.
Your mission hinges on unlocking the final code hidden within R2-D2's databanks, a code that holds the coordinates of a crucial location vital to the Rebel Alliance. As you embark on this journey of deciphering the droid's secrets, the success of your mission teeters on your ability to extract the desired information from this intrepid astromech.
